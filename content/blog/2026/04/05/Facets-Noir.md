I haven't done a BIPA for a few years, so this should make a pleasant change from my usual pale-n-hoppy beers.

When I went to choose the hops for this one I was very surprised to find that I didn't have anything new-worldy enough in stock - I had English hops like Fuggles and EKG, and European noble hops like Saaz, Tettnang, and Hallertau, but that was about it.

So the recipe as saved here is using a mix of Sorachi Ace and Bramling Cross in the boil and whirlpool, and currently has Citra for dry hopping - which remains to be determined, as I need to buy some new bags of hops and I'll pick one when I get to the dry hopping stage.

Hopefully the Bramling Cross will add some notes of dark fruits that should work with the slightly roasty flavours of the BIPA.
